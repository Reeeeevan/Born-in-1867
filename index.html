Frank Lloyd Wright the builder

Frank Lloyd Wright was an American architect who designed over 1,000 structures. Born in 1867, he believed in designing structures that were in harmony with humanity and its environment.

5 Unique Features of His Work

Organic Architecture

Buildings designed to complement their natural surroundings.

Open Floor Plans

Spaces that flow into one another without closed-off rooms.

Strong Horizontal Lines

Low, sweeping roofs that hug the landscape.

Custom Design Elements

Furniture and fixtures designed specifically for each building.

Innovative Materials

Use of concrete, glass, and steel in new ways.
